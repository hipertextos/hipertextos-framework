Animations

Hipertextos Framework

Animate it!

Some CSS3 animations from Dan Eden's Animate.css are integrated in hipertextos.css

Call animations adding "animated" and "animation-name" classes on your HTML elements.

You can do it directly:

<div class="animated bounce">Hello!</div> 
<div class="animated shake">Hello!</div> 
...

or with jQuery:

$('#element').addClass('animated bounce');
...
